My Final Project!

Spin-off of "Snake Game"

This is my final project, it
is called Slithery Snake. This is
basically a version of the old snake
game. You want to collect the apples
in order to increase the score and to
increase the length of the snake.
In order to play the game you simply
use the arrow keys to navigate around the
game. Be sure to avoid the walls and yourself
this will cause the game to end.
